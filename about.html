About JobSeekr

What is JobSeekr?

This application allows users to look for jobs in the location of their choice with specified criterias (e.g., by company). A job seeker is typically inundated with job openings that may not suit their preferences due to popular job seeking websites having millions of listings. This app allows the user to not be overwhelmed as they are recommended a curated selection of job postings matching their search criteria, all within a simple UI.

Limitations

Note that the Adzuna API has limited API calls/ day which may explain why no results show up for a basic search. In addition, it has limited capabilities and a select list of job information available.
